Kelby - The Visionary

Kelby Zorgdrager is a serial entrepreneur, a Sun Certified Java Programmer, developer, and architect who has recently delivered training to Apple, Yahoo, VMWare, Intuit, NetJets, and Shaw Communications. His expertise in Java, Open Source, and software development resulted in his invitations to guest speak at JavaOne, Java University, Comdex, IEEE Software Conference, and the Colorado Software Summit.

Driven by his passion to help people learn, Kelby has guest authored for IBM and Sun/Oracle’s developer sites, served as a technical editor for the bestselling book, Advanced Java: How to Program, and authored his own self-published book, Working with IntelliJ. Kelby’s belief in contextualized learning and his knack for entrepreneurship also led him to develop a training methodology to share with others who are equally as learning-hungry.
